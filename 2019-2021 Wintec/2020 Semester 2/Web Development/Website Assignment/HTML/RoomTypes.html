Hotel Room Types

This is the section the includes the room types that this hotel provides. under the navigation menu there is a drop down menu that will bring you to the desired room type.

Single Room

A room fit for one.
it includes a single bed and bathroom.
the single bed is situated in a sizeable room
allowing you to streach your legs from a long journey.

Double Room

The double room includes two single beds fit for multiple people per room.
This is perfect for a group traveling on a budget.

Queen Room

This room includes a massive queen bed and a sizeable room.
Moodlighting and

Studio Room

A small room that lacks in space but makes up for in price.

perfect for a travelling student.
